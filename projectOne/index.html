Easy Pancake Recipe
Scroll for instructions!
MIX!
Add milk & pancake mix to a large bowl and stir with a baking spatula or fork.
Stir!
Keep stirring until the texture is even and there are no more clumps.
Heat!
Place a 1 tbsp of butter into a pan and let it melt.
Pour!
Pour 1 cup of pancake batter into hot pan.
Cook!
Cook until golden brown.
Flip!
Flip the pancake and cook the other side!
Enjoy!
